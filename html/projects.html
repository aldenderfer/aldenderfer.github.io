Cycles of the Sky: NSF Space Grant -funded public outreach planetarium production, used in Howard University's planetarium for classes at both Howard and American University. (Audio and video production)

Electrical

ATLANTIS: quarter-scale military style tank through Make-a-Wish for an 11-year-old named Enzo. (In progress)

Arduino+Processing

Lightpoles: MIDI- / OSC- / liveaudio- controlled light poles. data transformed by Processing into RGB information and sent to Arduino, which PWMed to 6 sets of shift registers - individually indexing 16 RBG LEDs each, for a total of 48 outs per channel. Video.

Modelling the Solar System: Processing-based model of the bodies of our immediate solar neighborhood. All attributes (inclinations, orbits, eccentricities, etc) except the size of the sun are to scale. (With slight modification, this project can also be controlled with a Wii-nunchuck and an Arduino.)
