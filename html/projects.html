Arduino+Processing

Lightpoles: MIDI- / OSC- / liveaudio- controlled light poles. Data is transformed by Processing into RGB information and sent to Arduino, which PWMs to 6 sets of shift registers - for a total of 48 outs per channel. Video.

Modelling the Solar System: Processing-based model of the bodies of our immediate solar neighborhood. All attributes (inclinations, orbits, eccentricities, etc) except the size of the sun are to scale. (With slight modification, this project can also be controlled with a Wii-nunchuck and an Arduino.)
