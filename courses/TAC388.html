Course Structure

  • TAC 388 focuses on the end-to-end process of developing connected devices, from initial concept to functional prototype.
  • Students work either in teams or individually on a semester-long project to create a smart device that addresses a real-world problem.
  • Emphasis is placed on practical skills in hardware and software integration, user-centered design, and iterative development.
  • The course is organized around a series of milestones, including ideation and proposal, design, prototyping (fabrication, assembly, and testing), and final MVP.
