The Modular Robotic Arm is designed to be used as or with other assistive devices. It has ability to have modules added or removed in order to allow for a cheaper and more personalized assistance device. This will allow for a broader range of accessibility to persons who may require assistance in grabbing and moving objects with or without their own hands.
